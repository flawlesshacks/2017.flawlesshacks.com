FAQ

Who can attend?

Anyone who identifies as a woman or as gender non-conforming is welcome! If you are under 18, we’ll need a parental consent form.


Will you reimburse my travel?

Unfortunately, we will not be providing reimbursements for the hackathon.


I don't have a team, can I still participate?

Of course! It is absolutely not necessary to have a team prior to coming, and we can hook you up with a team when you get here.


How big can a team be?

Team sizes are flexible but we suggest a max of 5 people per team.


How do I get there?

Flawless Hacks will be held at Spotify NYC, 45 W 18th St in New York City.


Why do you need my resume?

We're looking forward to hooking you up with some awesome job and internship opportunities with our all star sponsors.


What if my hack isn't flawless?

We get this question all the time. Good news: you're flawless! So it ain't a problem.


You didn't answer my question!

We’re here for you! Send us an email at flawlesshackathon@gmail.com or feel free to reach out to us on Twitter or Facebook.
